About Me

As an MIT Innovator Under 35 in Europe for 2023 in AI, I'm dedicated to harnessing artificial intelligence to break down barriers and democratize technology. My mission is to create a more inclusive, connected world where everyone can communicate effectively and access cutting-edge tools.
